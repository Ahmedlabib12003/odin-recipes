Sloppy Joe

Description

Sleepy Joe is a cozy twist on the classic Sloppy Joe sandwich, designed as the perfect comfort food for evening meals. This hearty sandwich features ground beef simmered in a rich, mildly spiced sauce infused with chamomile tea and a hint of lavender honey, creating subtle calming notes. The meat mixture is enhanced with sautéed onions, garlic, and bell peppers, then seasoned with a blend of traditional tomato-based sauce and unique sleep-friendly herbs. Served on a toasted bun, each bite delivers a comforting combination of savory flavors with a gentle, soothing quality. It's a creative variation that maintains the satisfying essence of a Sloppy Joe while incorporating elements associated with relaxation and rest.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup brewed chamomile tea (cooled)
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons lavender honey (or regular honey)
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 6 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat
  2. Add onion and bell pepper, sauté until softened (about 5 minutes)
  3. Add garlic, cook for another minute until fragrant
  4. Add ground beef, break it up with a wooden spoon and cook until browned (about 8-10 minutes)
  5. Drain excess fat if needed
  6. Pour in the chamomile tea, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Stir to combine
  7. Add honey, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, paprika, black pepper, and salt
  8. Reduce heat to low, simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally until sauce thickens
  9. While the mixture simmers, lightly toast the hamburger buns
  10. Spoon the hot mixture onto the bottom buns and serve
